Shades of Pink

She writes in shades of pink.



She writes
in shades of pink

Unread

There's this little girl
That kept her writings hid
Everyone never knew
All the things she did

But lately she doesn't write
Scrambled words in mind
Silent and cold-eyed
She know that isn't right

She wonder what would it be like
To have reasons to write
How to escape form this locked cage?
In order to fill her blank page.
