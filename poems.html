Listening Stars

Not so long time ago, my heart was empty and alone.
Back then, I never met someone I could call my home.
Someone I could label as my happiness and as my own.
And will give such strength to this heart of mine that was torn.

I spent many sleepless nights staring at the sparkling stars.
I was hoping that the sights of them will help me mend my scars.
Hoping that as they invade the night, they'll help me forget the silent wars.
As I wished they'll get to hear my wishes although they're all too far.

I'd like to talk to them about my hopes of love.
I wonder if they listen for it's something I still don't have.
Back to those days, when my days were just too hard and rough.
And to finally meet someone who will make me feel enough.

And here you came, to answer my doubts and fade my fears.
You came right in time; I no longer have a reason for these tears.
You have no idea how I saw a new shade of light now that you are here.
In you, my struggles were taken, and my silence was something you want to hear.

The day you came, I knew right from there and then that you're here to stay.
I knew we met for a reason and that the world finally paved its way.
All stars finally aligned to make sure we won't go astray.
We found a love like stars belonged at night and how the sun is meant for days.

You are the answer, the pause, the start both my day in and out.
With you, I knew I am safe, and my sentiments always count.
Love, your presence is something I just can't live without.
You are my constant, and I knew this is what love is about.

Came and Stayed

Your love up to these days prohibited the pour of my rains.
You assured me that I'd never wake up with pain.
And suddenly, my words found its rhymes and my madness was tamed.
And that I should stop giving myself the blame.

You being here with me, made me realize that I have a place to go.
In your arms, they reminded me of what love is capable to do.
You accepted both my sunshine and glooms.
As you reminded me that you will never break my heart in two.

We make picture-perfect memories I could put in frames,
The excitements remained, and love remained in blazing flames.
I am still thankful that I have you and our chances came.
And the only thing I want to call is your name.

Your love glistened some of my dying hopes,
You write the words that I never spoke.
You loved all of my edges as if I was never torn and broke.
We surpassed the surging waves as long as we're on the same boat.

You became my game-changer, for once, the world never betrayed.
You stopped my clouds from turning grey.
You learned how to break my walls away.
You never stopped proving that you're here to stay.

You have me, and that means it'll go forever.
You know you mean so much to me, that's something you need to remember.
Your love made me lose count of days and the numbers.
You have my nights and days forever and I knew we'll make it together.

Towards Forever

From past to future I want you here, and I want you there.
You have all of me, and that goes everywhere.
I will bring you to my plans; I'll keep it-I swear.
You have me even on times you can't stand and bear.

I can't promise perfects days, for we will have inevitable fights.
We will have arguments questioning each other's rights.
We will have that stillness that would overcome some of our nights.
But we will grow from within until it vanishes our frights.

You're the only one I want to make memories with, that would last.
As stars keep sparkling, and the moon still casts.
We will take minutes slowly, and days with no rush.
From different windows and doors, we will watch the world adjust.

Adventures and happiness are what we will look forward.
May it be easy or even when days get hard.
Take all of it; take all my promises and every word.
We will spend more moments to share from this day onward.

We will spend more afternoons on coffees with as many poetries to read.
We will walk on new hallways hand in hand at a similar speed.
We will chase as many chances and dances on unfamiliar streets.
We will understand what there is as well as the languages that only our hearts speak.

We will have more stories to share, more answers to each other's "How?"
As years drag in, there's no need to fear what we have now.
All things about you, I am willing to learn and know.
And through rains and summers, together we will grow.
