Ben&Ben

Ben&Ben, formerly known as The Benjamins, is a folk pop/pop rock band in the Philippines. Formed in 2015, the band has since been known for their popular hits such as "Ride Home", "Leaves", "Maybe the Night", "Pagtingin", "Lifetime", "Doors", and the critically acclaimed "Kathang Isip". One of the most popular acts nationally, they are ranked as the number one most streamed Filipino artist on Spotify in 2020. They have won several awards, including multiple Awit Awards, Myx Awards, and Wish Awards. Prior to its formation as a band, the Guico twins, Paolo Benjamin and Miguel Benjamin Guico (then known as The Benjamins) were best known for writing the single "Tinatangi" (interpreted by Bayang Barrios and Cooky Chua featuring The Benjamins), which won 2nd runner up and Best Music Video at the PhilPop Music Festival in 2016. They formally changed their name in 2016 to Ben&Ben, a homage to their surname. The duo released their debut EP in late 2016, signing with major record label Warner Music Philippines. Expanding as an ensemble, the group became a 9-piece band on May 10, 2017, adding Poch Barreto on electric guitar, Jam Villanueva on drums, Agnes Reoma on bass, Patricia Lasaten on keyboards, Toni Muñoz and Andrew de Pano on percussions and back up vocals, and Keifer Cabugao on violin.
