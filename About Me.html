Jocelyn Dalusung



Jocelyn Dalusung's writing journey began when she started writing her own stories without letting anyone to read them. Her passion for writing been set aside when she decided to focus on playing chess. During high school, she became a campus journalist and a scriptwriter in an English radio broadcasting team. She hasn't continued writing something personal for quite a long time because she became an academic research writer.


Not until she met him. The man who made her want to write again.

Low Light

The page is still white
The ink is too light
And I still couldn't write
About how my soul dies

My heart slowly sank.
As I see, the page remained blank
Silently dying from the inside out
The unread life trickles out

Until you and I first met
The day I'll never forget
Dark nights became bright
As you turned the dark into light
